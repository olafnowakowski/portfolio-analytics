### Stakeholders at a utility company request insights summarizing the past month’s business results. The information will include high-level, historical data about electricity usage. What type of deliverable should the data team provide?
- They should provide a **report**. A report is a static collection of data given to stakeholders periodically. It typically contains high-level, historical data that is cleaned and sorted.

### A supervisor presents their data team with a clear objective that was set by company decision-makers and will be evaluated and measured. The data team then uses this objective to drive a work project. What does this scenario describe?
- This scenario describes **using a metric goal**. A metric goal is a measurable goal set by a company and evaluated using metrics.
