### What is the operator for a formula that uses division?
- A forward slash `(/)` is the operator for a formula that uses division.

### Fill in the blank: A cell _____ is composed of the letter of the cell’s column and the number of its row.
- A cell **reference** is composed of the letter of the cell’s column and the number of its row.

### What error message will appear when a formula cannot be interpreted as it is written? 
- The error message `ERROR` (also called a parsing error) will appear when a formula cannot be interpreted as it is written.

### What should be done to resolve a `NAME` error?
- To resolve a `NAME` error, **ensure the formula name is correct**.
