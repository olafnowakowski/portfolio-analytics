# Google Sheets Reflection Notes

## Question 1
**In Google Sheets, what access level should you select if you want your collaborator to comment on your spreadsheet, but not edit the content?**

**Answer:**  
**Can comment**

**Note:**  
This permission allows collaborators to leave feedback and suggestions without changing the spreadsheet data.

---

## Question 2
**What do you think are the main advantages of using Google Sheets to organize, analyze, and share your data?**

Google Sheets is easy to use for organizing, sorting, and analyzing data. It is also easy to share with others, can be accessed online from different devices, and supports collaboration in real time.

---

## Question 3
**How do you think the comment feature in Google Sheets can increase collaboration among teammates?**

The comment feature helps teammates give feedback without changing the original data. It makes collaboration easier because people can ask questions, suggest improvements, and explain what information should be updated directly inside the spreadsheet.
