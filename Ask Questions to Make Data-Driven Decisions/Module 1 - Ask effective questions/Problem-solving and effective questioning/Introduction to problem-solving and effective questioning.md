**Problem Solving and Questioning**
- Understand the importance of defining the problem clearly and aligning with stakeholder expectations to focus analysis.
- Learn to craft effective questions that guide data collection and lead to meaningful insights.

**Data Types and Tools**
- Explore different types of data and when each type is most useful for analysis.
- Discover how spreadsheets can be used effectively to organize and analyze data.

**Structured Thinking and Communication**
- Learn structured thinking to break down complex problems into smaller, manageable steps for logical solutions.
- Gain strategies for clear communication and managing stakeholder expectations to achieve business goals.
