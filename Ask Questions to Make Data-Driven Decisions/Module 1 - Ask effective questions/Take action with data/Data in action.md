# Ask
Anywhere Gaming Repair wanted to grow its business through advertising, but the owner did not know which type of advertising would work best. Maria, the data analyst, worked with stakeholders, including the owner, vice president of communications, and director of marketing and finance, to define the real problem: the company did not know its target audience’s preferred advertising method.

# Prepare
Maria gathered the data needed for the analysis. First, she identified the company’s target audience as people who own video game systems or accessories. Then she collected data about different advertising methods, such as TV, podcasts, radio, billboards, print, and public transportation ads, so she could compare which options were most effective for that audience.

# Process
Maria cleaned and organized the data to make it reliable for analysis. She removed errors, fixed inconsistencies, and transformed the data into a more useful format. This ensured the information was accurate and ready to support decision-making.

# Analyze
Maria analyzed the data to answer two key questions:
- Who is most likely to own a video gaming system?
- Where are these people most likely to see or hear advertisements?

Her analysis showed that people aged 18 to 34 were most likely to make video game-related purchases. She also found that TV commercials and podcasts were especially popular with this age group. Since the business had a limited budget, Maria determined that podcast advertising was the most cost-effective option.

# Share
Maria presented her findings and recommendation to the stakeholders using clear visuals and a simple explanation. She showed that podcast advertising would best reach the target audience while staying within budget. This helped the company understand the analysis and make a data-driven decision.

# Act
Based on Maria’s recommendation, Anywhere Gaming Repair partnered with a local podcast production agency to create a 30-second ad. The ad ran for one month, and the results were successful. The business saw an increase in customers after the first week and gained 85 new customers by the end of week four.
