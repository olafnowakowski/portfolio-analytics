### As a BA, to what business and organizational areas would you apply a SWOT Analysis? Select all that apply.
- Product development 
> Product development often involves conducting a SWOT Analysis to identify internal capabilities and external factors that may impact the success of new products.
- Vendor selection
> SWOT analysis can be used for vendor selection. When considering potential vendors, businesses can conduct a SWOT analysis to assess each vendor's strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats presented by the quality of their products, financial stability, relevant experience, potential long-term partnerships, scalability, geographical location, and existing alliances. 
- Strategic planning 
> Strategic planning is a typical application of SWOT Analysis, as it helps organizations evaluate their internal strengths and weaknesses and assess external opportunities and threats to develop strategic plans.

### Why is correct problem identification important in problem-solving processes?
- It ensures that the solution addresses the root cause of the problem so the problem does not reoccur.
> Correct problem identification is crucial because it allows for the development of solutions that target the problem's root cause, preventing problem recurrence.

### What is the primary purpose of the 5 Whys technique?
- To uncover the root cause of a problem by asking "why" multiple times.
> The primary purpose of the 5 Whys technique is to iteratively ask "why" to peel away the symptoms of a problem until the root cause is identified.
