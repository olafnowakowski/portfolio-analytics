# Topic: Gathering Requirements

## Scenario
You are planning a birthday party for your friend.  
To make sure the party is successful and enjoyable, you need to gather requirements from the people involved.

---

## Question 1: Identify Key Stakeholders

### Who would you want to speak to?

The key stakeholders could include:

- **Your friend**
  - The birthday person is the most important stakeholder because the party is for them.

- **Guests**
  - They can help you understand what activities, food, music, or location would make the party enjoyable.

- **Close friends or family of the birthday person**
  - They may know your friend’s preferences, schedule, favorite foods, and what kind of party they would enjoy.

- **Venue staff or host**
  - If the party is at a rented place or someone’s home, they can explain rules, capacity, timing, and setup options.

- **Caterer or food provider**
  - They can help with food options, dietary needs, budget, and timing.

- **Entertainment provider**
  - If you plan to have music, games, a DJ, or activities, this person can help with setup and requirements.

---

## Question 2: Stakeholder Requirements

### What questions could help you understand how to throw the best party?

You could ask questions such as:

- What kind of party would the birthday person enjoy most?
- Does the birthday person prefer a small gathering or a large party?
- What date and time works best for the birthday person and guests?
- What is the budget for the party?
- Where should the party take place?
- How many people should be invited?
- Are there any people who must be invited?
- What food and drinks should be served?
- Does anyone have dietary restrictions or allergies?
- What music or entertainment should be included?
- Should there be games, speeches, or planned activities?
- Does the birthday person like surprises, or should they know the plan?
- What decorations or theme would fit the party?
- How long should the party last?
- Are there any venue rules or limits?
- What supplies are needed, such as tables, chairs, speakers, or decorations?
- Who will help set up and clean up?
- How will guests be invited?
- What would make the party feel successful?

---

## Why These Questions Matter

These questions help you gather requirements by understanding:

- what the birthday person wants
- what the guests need
- what limits exist, such as budget, time, and location
- what resources are needed
- what would make the event successful

---

## Final Summary

Planning a birthday party is similar to gathering business requirements.  
You need to identify the stakeholders, ask the right questions, understand everyone’s needs, and use that information to plan a successful outcome.
