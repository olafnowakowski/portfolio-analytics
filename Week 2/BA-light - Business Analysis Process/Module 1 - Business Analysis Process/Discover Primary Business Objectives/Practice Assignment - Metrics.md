### What is a key performance indicator?
- A measure that tracks progress toward a business goal
> Key performance indicators (KPIs) are used to measure the performance of a company based on specific goals.

### True or false? All metrics are KPIs.
 - False
> While it is true that a KPI is a type of metric, not all metrics are KPIs.

### Let’s say that your company’s marketing department has created a social media post, and the goal of the post is to increase follower count (i.e., the number of people following your company on social media). Each of the measures below is a metric. Which one is a KPI?
- Follower growth from the previous day
> Tracking follower growth from the previous day will directly help the marketing department determine whether the post is helping them achieve their goal of increasing follower count.

### A ride-share company wants to focus on increasing customer satisfaction. Which of the following would be the most appropriate metric to use as a KPI?
- Number of customers taking repeat rides on the ride-share service
> If the number of customers taking repeat rides on the ride-share service is going up, that would be a good indicator of increased customer satisfaction.
