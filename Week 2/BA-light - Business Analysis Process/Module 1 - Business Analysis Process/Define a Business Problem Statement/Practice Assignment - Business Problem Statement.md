### What is the primary purpose of creating a problem statement?
- It allows you to determine the exact problem you want to solve through your business analysis process.
> A good problem statement guides your project and defines its scope.

- Can costs be reduced by sourcing ingredients from other suppliers?
- Can costs be reduced by changing the delivery process?

- Can profits be increased by raising the price of cupcakes and other baked goods?
