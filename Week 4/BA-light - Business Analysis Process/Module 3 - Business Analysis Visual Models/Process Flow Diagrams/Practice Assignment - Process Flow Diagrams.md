### Building a process flow diagram serves three main purposes. What are they? Select all three.
- Show a process from beginning to end
- Analyze how a process can be improved
- Break processes into easy-to-follow steps and symbols

### Which symbol is used to indicate the beginning or the end of a process?
- Oval
> An oval is usually used to indicate the beginning or the end of a process.

### Which symbol is used to indicate a decision that must be made?
- Diamond
> A diamond is used to indicate a decision that must be made. Depending on the answer (“yes” or “no”), a specific process will begin.

### True or false? When mapping a business process, it’s important to define the boundaries of the process you want to analyze and map. You need to focus on the specific process that you’re analyzing and hoping to improve; not on the processes that are not directly related to the business problem at hand.
True
> When mapping a business process, it’s important to define the boundaries of the process you want to analyze and map. You need to focus on the specific process that you’re analyzing and hoping to improve; not on the processes that are not directly related to the business problem at hand.
