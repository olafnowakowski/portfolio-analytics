### How do you best process new information? Do you prefer text, charts, or a combination of both? How might this apply to your work creating a document to describe a business analysis process?
- When creating a document for a **business analysis process**, here is how I apply that preference:
  - **Text for Governance**: I use concise text to define the who, why, and rules of the process. This ensures there is no ambiguity regarding roles or requirements.
  - **Charts for Workflow**: I use process maps (BPMN or simple flowcharts) to show the how and when. This allows a stakeholder to see the entire lifecycle of a project at a glance.
  - **Integration**: I typically "anchor" my visuals within the text. For example, a diagram will show the high-level steps, while the subsequent text provides the deep-dive details for each phase.
