### What is a business requirements document?
- A document shared amongst stakeholders to outline the scope of the analysis and the requirements as solutions are considered.
> A business requirements document outlines the scope of the analysis and the requirements as solutions are considered.

---

### Which of the following best describes how business requirements documents are used throughout a company?
- A BRD provides a touchstone for stakeholders to make decisions around priorities, timeline, and project management aligned with business goals.
> This answer accurately describes how businesses use BRDs.

### What is the purpose of a needs statement in a business requirements document?
- A needs statement clearly outlines the project’s priorities based on the specific requirements of stakeholders.
> This answer correctly describes the purpose of a needs statement.

---

### What is the purpose of an outcomes section in a business requirements document?
- An outcomes section highlights the specific KPIs and measures that will indicate if the solution is a success. 
> This answer correctly describes the purpose of an outcomes section. 

---

### Limitations such as budget, resource availability, deadlines, and external regulations would typically be found in which part of the BRD?
- Constraints
> The constraints section is the ideal place to list any limiting factors, which would impact any potential adjustments to the project.

### Which of the following statements about using jargon in a Business Requirements Document is true?
- Jargon should be avoided because it can distract the reader and take away from the central message of the document.
> Jargon can distract the reader, and therefore take away from the central thesis of the document.

---

### Why is specificity important when defining requirements for a business requirements document?
- Specificity ensures the team knows exactly what to deliver and prevents unnecessary work.
