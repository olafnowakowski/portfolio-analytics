### Once you know what information you need to share with your stakeholders in order to communicate your insights, what are three other things you should consider to effectively deliver the information to your audience? 
- Connect with your audience: Knowing your audience allows you to research what they value, which will help you curate your presentation accordingly.
- When it comes to data, stick to the facts: Refrain from statements that include judgment or assumptions.
- Use language that is easy to understand: Use clear, concise language. Don’t exaggerate, and maintain accuracy.
