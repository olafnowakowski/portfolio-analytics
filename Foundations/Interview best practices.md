# Job Search Advice for Data Analysts
- Reflect on specific instances where you used data to solve problems in professional or personal projects.
- Expand your professional network by updating your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles, connecting with other analysts, and joining local meet-ups.

# Interview Tips
- Prepare thoughtful, specific questions for interviewers to better understand the team and role.
- For case study interviews, focus on analyzing the provided data and linking your solution back to it, demonstrating your thought process.

# Proactive Job Application Strategies
- Beyond applying online, try to connect directly with recruiters or hiring managers through coffee chats or direct resume submissions.
- Persistence in reaching out can increase your chances of getting noticed and landing the job you want.
